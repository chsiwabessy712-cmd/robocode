🍰 What is Division?

Today we’re going to learn another POWERFUL concept in math and coding which is DIVISON.
Division means sharing something equally, refers to the act of separating something into parts

🤖 Why Is divison Important in Coding?

Divsion is a thinking tool that shows up everywhere in programming.
it help you to understand How can you split something into equal parts or figuring out patterns

👉 If you have 12 candies and 4 friends, division helps answer:
“How many candies does each friend get?”

🧠 Sharing (Fair Share)

You give out items one by one
until everything is shared equally.

Sharing Example:
10 cookies ÷ 2 kids = 5 cookies each

📦 Groups Model

Imageine like you make groups of equal size.

Group Example:
12 apples ÷ 3 apples per basket = 4 baskets

🔢 Division as Repeated Subtraction

You can imagine Division is like subtracting the same number again and again. it is about groups
and make you can separate something into small groups


Division
Repeated Substraction
12 ÷ 3 = 4 (we substract by three, 4 times)
12 - 3 - 3 - 3 - 3 = 0
10 ÷ 2 = 5 (we substract by two, 5 times)
10 - 2 - 2 - 2 - 2 - 2 = 0
16 ÷ 4 = 4 (we substract by four, 4 times)
16 - 4 - 4 - 4 - 4 = 0
18 ÷ 6 = 3 (we substract by six, 3 times)
18 - 6 - 6 - 6 = 0
