🎮 Movement using motors

A robot car usually has 2 motors,left motor to control left wheel and right motor to control right wheel
How the car moves depends on which motor is ON or OFF, and how fast it spins!

⬆️⬇️ Move forward and backward

The car can moves straight forward depend on the motor speed.

When both motor have same speed with positive value, it make the car move straight forward
But, when both motor have same speed with negative value, it make the car move straight backward

📌 Case forward :

Left Motor → ON
Right Motor → ON
Both motors spin forward at the same positive speed

🧠 Think of it like walking with both legs together

📌 Case backward :

Left Motor → ON (Backward)
Right Motor → ON (Backward)
Both motors spin forward at the same negative speed

🧠 Think of it like you step back with both legs together

⬅️➡️ Turn left, right, and spin around

Turning is a condition when car make a movement (forward or backward) to heading to another direction.
But, spinning is a condition when car try heading to another direction, but the position of the car still same.

To implement turning or spinning, the speed of both motors must be different.

📌 Turning case :

one Motor → OFF (or slow)
another Motor → ON (fast)
🔄 The car make a TURNING movement

🧠 Why? Because one wheel pushes more than another wheel

📌 Spinning case

Left Motor → Forward
Right Motor → Backward
🌀 The car SPINS in place like a dancer!

🧠 Because one wheel move forward & another move backward
