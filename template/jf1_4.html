📐 What is an angle?

Today we are going to learn about ANGLES. An angle is made when two lines meet at one point.
You can imagine when you drive a car an make a turn. An angle will represent how much you turn.

🧩 Types of Angles (From Small to Big!)

Let’s meet the angle family. Basicly, there's three type of angle

🟦 What Is a RIGHT ANGLE?

A right angle is a perfect corner angle. For a simple defenition, A right angle is an angle that looks like the corner of a square or a box.
take a kook around you!, example : Table corner, Door corner, Paper corner. All of it represent right angle
