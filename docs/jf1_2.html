🔢 What is Number Path?

A sequence number follows a clear order or pattern.
👉 The numbers come one after another, and you can predict what comes next.

⭐ Understanding Sequence and random

Sequence is something that come in order and have a pattern. but,
random is something that come unpredictable and not have any pattern

🧩 Sequence example :

A robot walks step 1 → step 2 → step 3
A game level goes Level 1 → Level 2 → Level 3
A timer counts 1, 2, 3, 4…

🎯 Random example :

Enemy appears in a random place🏴‍☠️
Dice game numbers 🎲
Surprise prize numbers 🎁

🔢 Positive & Negative Number Explorer

Positive is number that comes after 0 (zero) and bigger than zero (positive > 0)
Negative is number that come before 0 (zero) and less than zero (negative < 0)
